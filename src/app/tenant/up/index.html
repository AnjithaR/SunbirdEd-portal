About us

“Sahaj is our tribute to Our Teachers. It will be a companion in every aspect of a teacher’s teaching life, giving them the power to learn what they want, how they want, when they want and where they want. There have been various instances of teachers adopting technology organically, demonstrating an appetite for greater use of technology as a learning aid. Some have created digital teaching content, mobile applications, and web portals, and many actively participate in online discussion forums. The State has recognized this demand and developed Sahaj to empower teachers who are discovering the power of technology and are independently innovating with tech-based solutions in their own classrooms. Teachers can use Sahaj to create teacher learning material, share their goals, work, contributions and achievements. Sahaj’s tools and features empower Students and Parents to use Curriculum-linked resources and assessments. The platform presents a unique opportunity to leverage technology to leapfrog solutions to the benefit of teachers, students and parents in Uttar Pradesh”

VISION

Uttar Pradesh has a large number of talented and committed teachers who need to be celebrated and their expertise made available to others. In reality, the complexity and diversity of a classroom is only experienced by the teacher. In the absence of any formal redressal mechanisms, teachers often find organic solutions to deal with these challenges. One of the fundamental tenets of Sahaj is to enable teachers to create effective learning environments.

Sahaj’s tools and features empower teachers to find their own solutions by helping them create or use the materials they need and to customise them according to their contexts. For teachers to fulfil their central role as educators, they need easy access to teaching and learning resources and opportunities and motivation that are equally important for their professional development. In this context, Sahaj is an attempt to strengthen the capacity of teachers and to bring together all the efforts and re-imagine solutions at scale to positively impact the lives of all the teachers and learners in the State.

Objectives


  • To provide a support mechanism for the professional development of teachers aiding access to quality education for millions of learners in Uttar Pradesh.
  • To build solutions ranging from high-quality curriculum linked resources, teacher training content, assessment aids, teachers’ profile, announcements, teachers’ communities, etc. to empower teachers, students and parents.
